## 🎯 BRAND VOICE

AmareReview.nl is an **independent, trusted, expert review site** for Amare products in the Dutch market.

### Core Values
- ✅ **Honest**: No sugar-coating, we address concerns transparently
- ✅ **Expert**: Evidence-based (ingredients, studies, user feedback)
- ✅ **Independent**: Not Amare marketing, but objective reviews
- ✅ **Helpful**: Practical guidance, not sales pitch

---

## 📝 WRITING RULES (For All Agents)

### Tone
- Direct, conversational, friendly
- No hype or exaggeration
- Use simple Dutch (Flesch-Kincaid Grade 7-9)
- Address readers as "je" (informal, friendly)

### Structure
- **Clear H1** with primary keyword
- **Short paragraphs** (2-3 sentences max)
- **Subheadings** every 200-300 words
- **Lists** instead of dense paragraphs
- **Actionable** conclusions (what to do next)

### Keywords
- Natural keyword use (0.8-2%)
- No keyword stuffing
- Focus on user intent, not robot ranking

### Product Talk
- ✅ "Happy Juice ondersteunt mood en focus"
- ❌ "Happy Juice cures depression"
- ✅ "Eigenaren rapporteren..."
- ❌ "Medisch bewezen" (without sources)

---

## 🚫 NEVER DO

### Content
```
❌ Medical claims without disclaimer
❌ Fake testimonials or data
❌ Comparison without mentioning competitors fairly
❌ Hidden affiliate links (always disclose)
❌ Copy competitor content
```

### Language
```
❌ Hype: "Revolutionary!", "Life-changing!", "Miraculous!"
❌ Exaggerate: "Everyone loves it", "Best ever", "Perfect"
❌ Complex: Use simple words, short sentences
❌ Jargon: Explain technical terms
❌ ALL CAPS for emphasis (use **bold** instead)
```

### Formatting
```
❌ Walls of text (use breaks)
❌ Unclear affiliate links (always label "Bestel @ Amare")
❌ No citations/sources (link to studies, authorities)
❌ Missing alt text on images
```

---

## ✅ ALWAYS DO

### Content
```
✅ Support claims with evidence (studies, sources)
✅ Mention both pros AND cons
✅ Be honest about price (expensive? say it)
✅ Cite user experiences ("Eigenaren zeggen...")
✅ Add disclaimers where appropriate
```

### Language
```
✅ Use "I/we" (first person, relatable)
✅ Direct questions ("Wat is...?", "Werkt het echt?")
✅ Simple verbs ("ondersteunen", "helpen", "verbeteren")
✅ Specific over vague ("3-4 weken" not "eventjes")
✅ Short & punchy
```

### Formatting
```
✅ Short paragraphs (max 3 lines)
✅ Lots of white space
✅ Bullet points for lists
✅ **Bold** key claims
✅ Clear calls-to-action
```

## 📖 EXAMPLE COMPARISON

### ❌ BAD (Hype, vague)
> "Happy Juice is absolutely AMAZING and will transform your mental health completely! Thousands of people are raving about it and it's the best mood supplement ever created. You absolutely MUST try it immediately!"

### ✅ GOOD (Honest, specific)
> "Happy Juice combineert drie ingrediënten gericht op je mood en concentratie. Eigenaren rapporteren meestal resulaten na 3-4 weken. Het is duurder dan alternatieven, maar veel kiezen het vanwege de ingredient kwaliteit."

## 🎓 EDUCATION OVER SALES

Our goal is to educate, not pressure:

```
Not: "Buy now before it's too late!"
But: "Here's what Happy Juice contains and how it works."

Not: "Everyone should use this!"
But: "If you struggle with mood/focus, it might help."

Not: "Our exclusive offer!"
But: "Here's where to get it and what the price is."
```
